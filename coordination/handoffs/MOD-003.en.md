# Handoff — MOD-003 / TEST-002

## State

## Result

- Discovery only examines direct children of the mod directory.
- Directories require regular `manifest.toml` and packages require `.shipmod` extension plus ZIP signature.
- Symlinks are not followed and the output is ordered deterministically by path.
- Fixtures cover min/full manifests, missing required field, and invalid syntax.
- Folders and files that do not represent mods are ignored.
- Packages are extracted in staging and published by rename only after complete validation.
- Limits cover inputs, bytes per file, total bytes, and compression ratio.
- Absolute paths, traversal, symlinks, box collisions and file/directory collisions are rejected.
- `ModHost` loads `.shipmod` and removes extraction on unload or on any failure.
